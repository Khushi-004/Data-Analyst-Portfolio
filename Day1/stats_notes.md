# Day 1 - Stats Notes

## Mean 
Total sum ko kitne number hain usse divide karo.
Example: 10, 20, 30 ka mean = 60/3 = 20

## Median 
Sabse pehle ascending order me lagao.
Phir beech wala number utha lo.
Outlier ho to Median use karte hain kyunki ye effect nahi hota.

## Mode 
Jo number sabse zyada baar repeat ho.
Example: 45, 45, 82 
Mode = 45
